My Projects

The inspiration for this project came from one of my favorite games ever: Battlefield 1. It sparked my interest in the First World War, and it is something I highly recommend playing. One of the greatest inventions of the war was the tank, as it allowed the allies to cross No Man's Land with relative ease. The latest of the British tanks, the most advanced one, was the Mk. V tank. I decided to remake it in Tinkercad, and it is the project I am most proud of.
