The Krystorious cheap, but effective

Let me get this out of the way, this catapult is about as durable as it looks. It is a super simple arduino setup, using only a few wires, a button, and a motor. The catapult arm was held together with string and hot glue. It is very ugly, there's no denying that. Despite all the flaws, this abomination still managed to be one of the most reliable designs in my class, as it was able to launch a ball over a net with a 100% success rate. It's ugly, but I'm still mildly proud of it.
